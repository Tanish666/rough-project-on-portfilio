Services

Consultation and Site Assessment

  • Initial consultation to understand the client's energy needs and goals.
  • Site assessment to determine the feasibility of installing a solar system.
  • Analysis of roof space, shading, and structural integrity.

System Design and Engineering

  • Custom design of the solar power system tailored to the client's property.
  • Electrical and structural engineering services to ensure system safety and efficiency.
  • Selection of appropriate solar panels, inverters, and other components.

Installation

  • Professional installation of solar panels, inverters, and other system components.
  • Wiring and connection to the electrical grid or battery storage systems.
  • Quality assurance and safety checks throughout the installation process.

Customer Education and Support

  • Educating clients on how to monitor and maintain their solar systems.
  • Providing ongoing customer support and answering questions about system operation.

Repair and Troubleshooting

  • Diagnostic services to identify and fix issues with the solar system.
  • Repair or replacement of faulty components.
  • Emergency repair services for urgent problems.

Maintenance and Monitoring

  • Regular maintenance services to ensure optimal system performance.
  • Monitoring services to track energy production and detect potential issues.
  • Cleaning of solar panels and inspection of system components.
